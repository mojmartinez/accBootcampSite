HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

The 24-week ACC Software Development Bootcamp costs $7,200.

Details can be found here.

WHAT DAY & TIME ARE CLASSES HELD?

We designed this bootcamp with working folks in mind. Classes are held at night and on weekends so you can enroll without quitting your day job.
Depending on your cohort you will either have class Monday (7p-10p), Wednesday (7p-10p) and Saturday (9a-1p) or you will have class Tuesday (7p-10p), Thursday (7-p-10p) and Saturday (9a-1p).

HOW DO I KNOW IF THIS IS RIGHT FOR ME?

    Here are the top things you can do to decide if this is right for you:
  • Check out our syllabus.
  • Schedule a free one-on-one phone call with one of our instructors. It's not a sales call. We are here to help.
  • Enroll in the 2-day Trial Class. The Trial Class will give you a taste for the pace, instructor style and teach you the basics of HTML, CSS and jQuery!
  • Keep reading through the FAQs.

WHAT HAPPENS IF I'M SICK OR OUT OF TOWN? CAN I ATTEND CLASS VIRTUALLY?

You may attend class via live video conference.

I'M READY! HOW CAN I PREPARE?

    Great! Here's how to prepare:
  • If you haven't already, be sure to enroll in the next upcoming 2-Day Trial Class. This is a great way to learn the basics and get to know your instructors.
  • Schedule a time to chat with one of our instructors by phone.

WHAT'S COVERED?

This is a Full Stack bootcamp, meaning we cover front end and back end technologies. You'll learn: Git, HTML, CSS, jQuery, JavaScript, NodeJS, Express, SQL, MongoDB, React, TDD. To view all topics, check out our syllabus.

HOW MANY HOURS PER WEEK SHOULD I EXPECT TO SET ASIDE?

About 20-30 hours in total. Expect to spend 10 hours per week in class (ACC Highland Business Center Campus) and 15-20 hours outside of class on homework.

WILL I RECEIVE A CERTIFICATE FOR COMPLETING THE CLASS?

You will receive an ACC Certificate of Completion.
